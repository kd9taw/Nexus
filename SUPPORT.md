# Getting help with Tempo

Thanks for using Tempo! Here's the fastest way to get unstuck or get involved.

> Reminder: Tempo is **beta** — the FT1/DX1 waveforms are validated by
> simulation, not yet on the air. Real-world results are expected to vary, and
> reporting them helps a lot (see below).

## 1. Check the manual first

The **[operator manual](docs/manual/)** is your full guide:

- **[Getting Started](docs/manual/Getting-Started.md)** — install + first run.
- **[Troubleshooting](docs/manual/Troubleshooting.md)** — blank window, SmartScreen, no decodes, CAT/rigctld, audio levels, time sync, PTT.
- **[Rig & Audio Setup](docs/manual/Rig-and-Audio-Setup.md)** and the **[FAQ](docs/manual/FAQ.md)**.

## 2. Ask a question

For setup help, usage questions, or general discussion, open a
**[Discussion](https://github.com/kd9taw/tempo/discussions)**. Quick on-air
chatter is welcome there too.

## 3. Report something

- **Bug?** Open a [bug report](https://github.com/kd9taw/tempo/issues/new?template=bug_report.md).
- **On-air result (success or failure)?** Open an [on-air report](https://github.com/kd9taw/tempo/issues/new?template=on_air_report.md) — this is the data the project needs most.
- **Idea?** Open a [feature request](https://github.com/kd9taw/tempo/issues/new?template=feature_request.md).
